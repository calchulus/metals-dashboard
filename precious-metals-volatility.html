Methodology & Sources

Note: Realized volatility is computed as the annualized standard deviation of daily log-returns over a rolling window. Data derived from spot price histories (London Bullion Market, LME, NYMEX, MCX) and cross-referenced with published research from World Gold Council, Metals Focus, Johnson Matthey, and USGS. Base metals and rare earths included where commodity-grade spot data exists. Volatility estimates reflect historical patterns up to Q2 2026 and may not predict future realized vol.
