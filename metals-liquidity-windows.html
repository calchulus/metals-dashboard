Methodology

Note: Liquidity profiles derived from historical volume data across LBMA, COMEX/NYMEX, LME, SHFE, and MCX. Volume patterns are normalized to 0-100% scale per exchange. Dead zones represent periods with <5% of peak volume. Overlap windows show simultaneous trading across 2+ major exchanges — optimal for arbitrage but also when gaps close. Gap risk is highest during session transitions where price discontinuities can exceed 1-2% in thin markets. Fees are indicative and vary by broker/institution. Currency risk reflects historical volatility and hedging costs.
